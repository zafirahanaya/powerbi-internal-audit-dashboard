# 📊 Internal Audit Risk Monitoring Dashboard

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** developed to monitor internal audit findings, assess risk exposure, analyze financial impact, and support data-driven decision making.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes internal audit findings collected from multiple business units and departments. The dashboard enables management to monitor audit performance, identify high-risk areas, evaluate outstanding findings, and understand the financial impact of audit issues through interactive visualizations.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Monitor total audit findings across the organization.
- Identify departments with the highest audit findings.
- Analyze audit findings by risk level.
- Evaluate outstanding findings requiring management attention.
- Assess financial impact across business areas.
